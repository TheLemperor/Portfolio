MECHANICAL WINCH

POE: 10/17/2018 - 10/22/2018

The problem was to design and build a winch that was able to lift 100 grams at least 30 centimeters over the side of a table. The parts that would be used were either Fishertek or VEX parts. The motor had to be Fishertek and had to have an on and off switch. A netlogo program was also needed to simulate the winch.

In this project, I had a huge impact with the designing and physical building of the winch. I did a majority of the designing, while my partner and I equally assembled the parts.

STRUCTURAL DESIGN

POE: 3/7/2019 - 3/19/2019

The problem of this project was to use the Westpoint bridge designer software to design and test a bridge that is both stable and cost efficient. This meant that research had to be done, and different member and joint placements were played around with. Not only that, but the materials and widths of the members were also changeable.

For this project, I created 2 prototype bridges that were both cost efficient and stable. I spent multiple classes figuring out a bridge that I thought worked the best. Not only this but I covered a majority of the documentation and background research.
